## 5. Payment Terms

### 5.1 Pricing

Service prices are displayed on our Platform and include:
- **Service Fee**: The base fee for the handyman service
- **Platform Fee**: A 10% fee charged on top of the Service Fee

**Example Pricing:**
| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Service Fee | $100.00 |
| Platform Fee (10%) | $10.00 |
| **Total Amount** | **$110.00** |

### 5.2 Payment Processing

- All payments are processed securely through Stripe
- Payments are held in escrow until job completion is confirmed
- We accept major credit cards and debit cards

### 5.3 Payment Breakdown and Fees

When a Customer pays for a service, the Total Amount is distributed as follows:

| Recipient | Percentage | Description |
|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Payment Processor (Stripe) | ~3.4% + $0.50 | Third-party processing fee |
| EazyDone Handyman | ~9% of net | Platform fee for services |
| Handyman | ~88% of net | Service fee for work performed |

**Detailed Example ($110 Total):**
| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Customer Pays | $110.00 |
| Stripe Processing Fee | -$4.24 |
| Net Amount | $105.76 |
| Platform Fee (from net) | $9.61 |
| **Handyman Receives** | **$96.15** |

*Note: Stripe processing fees may vary based on card type and region.*

### 5.4 Escrow and Fund Release

1. **Payment Authorization**: When a Customer confirms a job, payment is authorized and held securely.
2. **Job Completion**: The Handyman marks the job as complete upon finishing the work.
3. **Customer Confirmation**: The Customer confirms job completion via WhatsApp notification.
4. **Fund Release**: Upon confirmation, funds are released to the Handyman's account.

### 5.5 Payout Schedule

- Handymen receive payouts to their linked bank account
- Payouts are processed within 2-3 business days after fund release
- Minimum payout thresholds may apply

## 6. Job Process

### 6.1 Job Creation

1. Customer selects a service category
2. Customer provides job details and location
3. Customer reviews pricing and confirms booking
4. Payment is authorized and held in escrow

### 6.2 Job Assignment

1. Available Handymen can view and accept jobs
2. Customer may be notified of interested Handymen
3. Job is assigned to a qualified Handyman

### 6.3 Job Completion

1. Handyman performs the requested service
2. Handyman marks the job as complete
3. Customer receives confirmation request
4. Customer confirms completion or reports issues
5. Upon confirmation, payment is released

### 6.4 Job Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Pending | Job created, awaiting Handyman |
| In Progress | Handyman assigned, work underway |
| Pending Confirmation | Awaiting Customer confirmation |
| Completed | Job finished, payment released |
| Disputed | Issue reported, under review |
| Cancelled | Job cancelled |
